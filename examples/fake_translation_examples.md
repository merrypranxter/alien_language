# Fake Translation Examples

Oraphine is not intended to be directly translatable, but here are playful pseudo-translations to spark imagination.

- A common glyph followed by a forked glyph and a crescent-marked glyph might mean *"The vessel awakens"*.
- An elevated glyph with a halo and crown next to three dotted glyphs could represent *"Council of the Nine"*.
- A sigil sealed with a ring of lower nodes might signify *"Portal to the realm of light"*.

Such interpretations are purely artistic and should not constrain creative use.
