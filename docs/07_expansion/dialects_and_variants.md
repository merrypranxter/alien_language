# Dialects and Variants

As Oraphine evolves, you may explore variations or dialects. Guidelines:

- Keep the core construction units but alter proportions, accent placement or curvature.
- Introduce new motifs sparingly and derive them from existing components.
- Create dialect names that reflect thematic shifts (e.g., *Corrupted*, *Ceremonial*, *Interface*).
- Document differences clearly and store variant inventories in separate JSON files or subfolders.

Variants enrich the language without diluting its identity.
