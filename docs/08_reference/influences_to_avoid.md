# Influences to Avoid

To preserve the uniqueness of Oraphine, avoid leaning too heavily into these sources:

- **Aurebesh and generic sci‑fi fonts:** they are too familiar and mechanical.
- **Ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs:** their pictorial nature conflicts with Oraphine's abstract shapes.
- **Nordic runes:** their straight lines and angles do not match Oraphine's mixture of curves and geometry.
- **Japanese kana and syllabaries:** their stroke order and simplicity differ from Oraphine's stacked structure.
- **Occult sigil alphabets:** they often rely on scribbles and loops rather than structured geometry.

While inspiration can be drawn from many sources, the script must remain visually distinct.
