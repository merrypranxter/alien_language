# Motif Index

Common motifs that recur throughout Oraphine:

- **Eye chamber:** an eye‑shaped enclosure with a vertical or diagonal stem.
- **Split crescent:** a crescent divided into two segments.
- **Forked tail:** a split lower terminal on a stem or chamber.
- **Broken halo:** a ring that is open or offset.
- **Triangular crown:** a wedge placed on top of a stem or chamber.
- **Twin nodes:** two dots placed either above, below or aside the main body.
- **Hanging barb:** a downward tick or small wedge suspended beneath a glyph.
- **Nested oval:** a smaller chamber inside a larger enclosure.
