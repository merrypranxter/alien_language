# Glossary

A quick reference of terms used in this repo:

- **Anchor:** the main structural element of a glyph (stem, chamber or wedge).
- **Chamber:** an enclosed oval, eye or capsule shape.
- **Crescent:** an open arc or hook.
- **Divider mark:** a punctuation‑like symbol separating clusters of glyphs.
- **Elevated glyph:** a more complex symbol used for names, titles or sacred words.
- **Fork:** a split tail or bifurcated terminal.
- **Halo:** a floating ring or broken circle marking sacredness or cosmic status.
- **Node:** a filled dot used as an accent.
- **Sigil:** a large seal‑like glyph used for rituals or emphasis.
- **Structural part:** the main body or skeletal component of a glyph.
