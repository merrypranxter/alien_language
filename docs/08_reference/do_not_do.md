# Do Not Do

Stylistic pitfalls to avoid when designing or using Oraphine:

- Overly calligraphic lines with variable stroke width.
- Runic straight strokes without any curves.
- Direct reuse of Arabic, Hebrew, Cyrillic or other real scripts.
- Perfectly symmetrical logos or icons.
- Decorative flourishes that do not reuse existing motifs.
- Uniform spacing and alignment that makes the writing look typographic.
