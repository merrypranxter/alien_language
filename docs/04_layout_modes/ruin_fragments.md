# Ruin Fragments

Ruin fragments show Oraphine inscriptions partially destroyed or eroded. Guidelines:

- Use incomplete glyphs with missing strokes or broken chambers.
- Repeat motifs irregularly to indicate damage and age.
- Insert broken rings and corrupted slashes to signal decay.
- Combine with textured surfaces like stone, metal or bone to enhance authenticity.

Ruin fragments are perfect for archaeological scenes and to convey the passage of time.
