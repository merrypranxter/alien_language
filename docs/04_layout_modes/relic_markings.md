# Relic Markings

Relic markings wrap Oraphine inscriptions around three‑dimensional objects. Use them on artifacts, weapons, statuettes and devices. Guidelines:

- Follow the contours of the object: wrap bands around cylinders, carve into edges or embed panels.
- Repeat short clusters for labels; avoid long continuous text unless it's part of a design motif.
- Combine text bands with sigils or circular seals for a layered look.
- Distress or erode the markings to suggest age.

Relic markings should feel like functional labels or incantations left by an advanced, ancient culture.
