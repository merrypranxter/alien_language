# Etched Mode

Etched mode depicts Oraphine as carved or scratched into hard surfaces such as stone or metal. Characteristics:

- Lines are slightly rough and irregular, with chipped edges.
- Some strokes are broken or faded to simulate wear.
- Raised or recessed carving adds depth and shadow.
- Occasional moss or patina can accentuate age in artwork.

Use etched mode for monuments, ruins and relics to convey antiquity.
