# Modifier Meanings

Certain small marks in Oraphine have loose semantic biases:

- **Top dot:** activation, tone or charged state.
- **Double top dot:** plurality, chorus or amplification.
- **Lower dot:** matter, grounded form or materiality.
- **Thin slash:** negation, damage or forbidden quality.
- **Side tick:** grammatical inflection or relational tweak.
- **Mini crescent above:** celestial or psychic modifier.
- **Wedge crown:** authority, title or command.
- **Fork tail:** motion, broadcast or unfolding force.

These meanings are fuzzy by design and can vary depending on context, but repeating these associations helps create the illusion of a structured language.
