# Stroke Logic

The strokes of Oraphine are neither calligraphic flourishes nor rigid stencils. Use these guidelines:

- **Weight:** draw most lines at a medium thickness; use thinner connectors sparingly.
- **Filled shapes:** fill dots and wedges to distinguish them from open lines.
- **Curves and straights:** combine geometric lines with flowing arcs to create tension between mechanical and organic.
- **No brush variation:** avoid tapering strokes or variable width calligraphy; Oraphine is drawn with a uniform tool.
