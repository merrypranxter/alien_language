# Construction Units

The Oraphine script is built from a limited set of structural and accent parts.

## Structural parts

- **Stem:** a vertical, diagonal or slightly curved line that acts as the main support.
- **Chamber:** an enclosed oval, eye or capsule shape representing containment or identity.
- **Wedge:** a triangular or shard‑like form suggesting direction, command or division.
- **Crescent:** an open arc or hook indicating energy, moon, psyche or subtle forces.
- **Fork:** a split tail or bifurcated end conveying motion, emission or branching.

## Accent parts

- **Node:** a dot or filled circle adding emphasis, count or intensity.
- **Bar:** a crossbar, tick or slash marking negation, corruption or grammatical distinction.
- **Halo:** a floating ring or broken circle denoting sacredness, status or cosmic relation.

All glyphs in Oraphine are assembled by combining one structural anchor with one or more of these bodies and accents.
