# Asymmetry Rules

Asymmetry is essential to the alien feel of Oraphine. While many glyphs suggest symmetry, they should never be perfectly mirrored. Follow these guidelines:

- Offset one side's accent: place a dot or tick on only one side of the stem.
- Tilt split elements: lean a fork slightly, or make one prong longer.
- Vary crescents: draw one crescent thicker or larger than the opposite one.
- Shift halos: place floating rings off‑center or break them so they misalign.

These subtle disruptions of balance make the writing system feel intentional but inhuman.
