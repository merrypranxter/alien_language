# Usage Philosophy

Oraphine is not a direct cipher or conlang. It is a visual language designed for artistic deployment. Use it as:

- **Relic inscriptions and temple markings:** long bands of text or vertical columns carved into stone and metal.
- **Circular seals and sigils:** radial compositions around a central chamber or node.
- **Interface labels:** short strings of glyphs on biotech dashboards and sacred machines.
- **Decorative bands:** ornamental strips on posters, sculptures or digital art.

When incorporating Oraphine into art, vary the density, scale and layout of the script to create rhythm and authenticity. Avoid mixing unrelated symbol families or clean typographic alignment—embrace imperfection and ritual variation.
